### RACI Legend
- **R** — Responsible: performs the work
- **A** — Accountable: final decision authority
- **C** — Consulted: provides input before the decision
- **I** — Informed: notified after the decision

### Confidentiality Clause (standard)
All information exchanged between the parties in connection with this SOW shall be treated as confidential and shall not be disclosed to third parties without prior written consent, except as required by law.

### IP Clause (work-for-hire, standard)
All deliverables produced under this SOW shall be considered work-for-hire. Upon full payment, all intellectual property rights transfer to the Client, excluding pre-existing IP and third-party components which remain licensed under their original terms.

### Warranty Clause (standard)
The Vendor warrants that all deliverables will conform to the acceptance criteria specified in this SOW for a period of [DURATION] from final acceptance. During the warranty period, the Vendor will correct any defects at no additional cost.
